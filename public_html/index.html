Warehouse Game

How To Play

  • Push the Treasure Chest back to the door with as few moves and explosions as possible
  • Do not push a numbered crate into the door, or else, you lose
  • Do not press space while pointed at the Treasure Chest, or else, you lose

    • How To Move: (Only 90 Degree Turns)

  • Explode Crate: Space Bar +100 to score
  • Move Bobcat (arrow keys): +1 to score when pressed
  • Maximum Crate Push Weight: Three (3) of any weighted-crate combination
  • Move Up: Up-Arrow
  • Move Left: Left-Arrow
  • Move Right: Right-Arrow
  • Move Down: Down-Arrow
